Description

Nested within a grove of woods, Eastwood Apartments features recently rennovated interiors and privacy. Each unit features a full kitchen, including a sink, garbage disposal, dishwasher, stovetop oven, microwave, and refrigerator. The community is located near major roads and highways, providing convenient access to schools, stores, and food.
